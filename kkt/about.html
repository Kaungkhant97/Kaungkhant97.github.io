About Our Business

Founded in August of 2008 and based in Shwe Taung gyar, Yangon, Travelme.com is a trusted community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book unique destination around the Myanmar — online

Whether it is travelling by express, flight or hiking for a day, a castle for a week, or a villa for a month, Travelme.cpm connects people to unique travel experiences, at any price point, in more than 20 cities. And with world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Travelme.com is the easiest way for people to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of millions.
