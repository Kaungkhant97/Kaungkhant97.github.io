  • Myanmar is emerging from decades of isolation, and as such it is more conservative than nearby countries. Many people still wear traditional dress â€“ the longyi for men, the htamein for women, both sarong-like garments. You rarely see anyone expose their knees or shoulders, and you will make everyone more comfortable if you follow suit.

  • People eat something like green leaves called "KwanYar". Various integridents are wrapped inside that leaves. After grinding KwanYar, people throw out the red liquid look alike blood. Because of eating KwanYar a lot, some men's tooth turn red. If you see some things like blood in Myanmar people's mouth, don't be afraid. They are just eating Kwanyar. There is no vampire in Myanmar.

  • Money is handed over and received with the right hand, while the left hand loosely supports the right arm.

  • Never use your feet to point at a person or thing.

  • A smile always goes a long way, as does knowing a few words in Burmese.
